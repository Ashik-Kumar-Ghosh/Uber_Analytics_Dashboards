# 🚖 Uber Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive **Uber Analytics Dashboard** designed to analyze ride bookings, revenue performance, customer behavior, and vehicle usage. The dashboard provides interactive insights into operational metrics such as completed rides, cancellations, revenue trends, and customer segmentation.
This project demonstrates **data visualization, business intelligence, and analytical reporting** using interactive dashboards.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Track **completed, cancelled, and lost bookings** across months.
- Visualize **monthly and quarterly revenue trends**.
- Compare **vehicle type performance** (Auto, Bike, Go Mini, Go Sedan, Premier Sedan, Uber XL, eBike).
- Analyze **customer segments** (first-time, return, and regular riders).
- Identify **top pickup and drop locations**.
- Evaluate **payment method distribution** (UPI, Cash, Wallet, Credit/Debit Cards).
- Monitor **driver and customer ratings** for service quality.
- Provide **time-slot booking heatmaps** to optimize demand forecasting.

---

## 📊 Key Features
- **Top Metrics Summary**: Completed bookings, lost bookings, revenue, total distance, and average distance.
- **Booking Status Indicators**: Completed, Cancelled, Incomplete.
- **Revenue Analysis**:
  - By month and quarter.
  - By vehicle type.
  - By payment method.
  - By customer.
- **Customer Insights**:
  - Rider categories (first-time, return, regular).
  - Customer-level booking and distance details.
- **Operational Insights**:
  - Location-based distance and booking trends.
  - Cancel ride reasons.
  - Ratings dashboard (customer vs. driver).
- **Visualizations**:
  - Line graphs for bookings.
  - Bar charts for revenue.
  - Heatmaps for time-slot demand.
  - Tables for customer-level details.

---

## 📂 Dashboard Modules
1. **Home** – Entry point with navigation and high-level KPIs.  
2. **Overview** – Consolidated metrics and trends across bookings, revenue, and distance.  
3. **Vehicle** – Performance by vehicle type, contribution %, and distance/location analysis.  
4. **Revenue** – Monthly & quarterly revenue, breakdown by vehicle type and payment method.  
5. **Rider** – Rider segmentation (first-time, return, regular) and customer-level booking details.  
6. **Location** – Top pickup and drop points, distance analysis by region.

## 📈 Insights Example
- **Auto-rickshaw rides** generate the highest revenue contribution (~25%).  
- **UPI payments** dominate with ~45% of total revenue.  
- **Peak demand** occurs between **6 PM – 9 PM on Sundays**.  
- **Customer ratings (4.40)** slightly exceed **driver ratings (4.23)**, suggesting service quality improvements.
